Frequent questions

You have questions and we have answers

Our team of credible teachers can give you the support you need.

How many projects do we get?

You'll receive seven projects throughout the duration of your cohort. Each project is reviewed by a teacher and presented to your classmates.

What happens after I enroll?

After you enroll in your cohort, you'll receive an email with an orientation package.

Can I take more than one cohort at once?

Each cohort will take five to ten hours of your time each week to complete the live classes and homework.

How will I meet my classmates?

In your orientation package, you'll receive access to a class chat in slack where you can talk to all of your classmates and teachers.

How many students in a cohort?

Each cohort will have a group of 15 students per class. This allows our teachers to give you the attention you need to learn best.

Is Jobbyist® a real company?

Nope, but it's a super-duper cool free website template for everyone on earth to use.

How time intensive are each cohort class?

Each cohort will take five to ten hours of your time every week to complete the live classes and homework.

Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes, you can receive a full refund up until your cohorts first class begins.
